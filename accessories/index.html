Accessories Management

Standalone accessories management app with Realtime sync and simplified inventory control.

View Changelog

Overview

The Accessories Management app allows independent control of accessory inventory, product updates, and stock tracking in a simple and responsive interface.

Realtime Database

Automatic updates across all connected devices using Firebase Realtime Database.

Inventory Tracking

Manage stock, check low inventory alerts, and perform quick product lookups.

Smart Operations

Seamless add, edit, and delete functions with fast, modern UI elements.

Unified Login

Shared authentication with Mobifixer for cross-app access and management.
